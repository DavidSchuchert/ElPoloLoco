Methods

checkCollisions()

Checks and handles various types of collisions within the game: - Collision between the character and enemies. - Collision between throwable bottles and the endboss. - Collision between the character and collectible bottles. - Collision between the character and collectible coins. When collisions are detected, this method will: - Remove killed enemies from the game. - Adjust health bars and status bars based on interactions. - Play or pause sound effects based on the state of the game. - Remove collected items from the game world and increment inventory counters. - End the game when certain conditions are met (e.g., character energy reaches zero).
